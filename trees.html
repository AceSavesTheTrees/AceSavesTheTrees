Trees are very important for people, animals, and the Earth.

Facts About Trees

  • Trees give us oxygen to breathe.
  • Trees clean the air by removing pollution.
  • Trees provide homes for animals and birds.
  • Trees help keep the Earth cool.
  • Trees help prevent flooding and soil erosion.
  • Trees give us fruit, wood, and shade.

Without trees, life on Earth would be very difficult.
